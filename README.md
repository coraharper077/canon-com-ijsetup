# ij.start.canon – Canon Printer Setup Guide

# <p><a href="https://sites.google.com/view/ijstart-canoncom/"><img src="/get started button.png" alt="canon com ijsetup" /></a></p>

Setting up your Canon printer is simple with <a href="https://github.com/coraharper077/canon-com-ijsetup/">ij.start.canon</a>. This official Canon support portal provides everything you need — from driver downloads to installation instructions — ensuring your printer is ready in minutes.

<h3>Step‑by‑Step Setup Process</h3>
Visit the Portal  
Go to ij.start.canon in your browser.

<h3>Choose Your Printer Model</h3>
Enter your Canon printer model to access the correct drivers and manuals.

<h3>Download Drivers & Software</h3>
Select your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux) and download the recommended drivers.

<h3>Install the Software</h3>
Run the installer and follow the on‑screen instructions to complete setup.

<h3>Connect Your Printer</h3>

USB Connection: Plug in the cable and let the system detect your printer.

Wireless Setup: Use the Wi‑Fi setup option to connect your printer to the network.
